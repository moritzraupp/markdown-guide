## Headings


```md
# Heading 1
## Heading 2
### Heading 3
#### Heading 4
##### Heading 5
###### Heading 6
```
Result:

# Heading 1
## Heading 2
### Heading 3
#### Heading 4
##### Heading 5
###### Heading 6

---

```md
Alternative Heading
===

Alternative Sub-Heading
---
```
Result:

Alternative Heading
===

Alternative Sub-Heading
---

Avoid this type of Heading, since  `---` is also used to make horizontal lines. Always leave blank lines between sections to avoid problems.

---

## Paragraphs and Line Breaks

To create paragraphs, use a blank line to separate one or more lines of text.

To create a line break or new line (\<br\>), end a line with two or more spaces, and then type return. In some renderers, this is not needed.
